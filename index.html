Games are on the weekend almost weekly at various suburb rinks. Come join us!

The University of Chicago Women's Ice Hockey Team has been a club team for more than ten years. We play various other university and recreational teams in the Chicagoland area and beyond; we are open to any interested university-affiliated women of all skill levels. Beginners are strongly encouraged to join.

To join the team or schedule a scrimmage or game, send an email to the team captain Maddie at madeline.sovie@gmail.com.
